02 — About

Who I am

← Replace with your photo

I'm Andrii Teslia — a Senior Product & UX/UI Designer with 8 years of experience building products that real people enjoy using. I currently work at Newsoft as an AI Experience Designer on a fast-growing health & wellbeing platform for the UK market.

My work spans fintech, proptech, health-tech, B2B SaaS, and CRM products. I pair Figma mastery — team libraries, design tokens, auto-layout — with deep fluency in Apple HIG, Material Design, and WCAG 2.2. I architect design systems from scratch, mentor junior designers, and leverage AI-assisted workflows (Cursor, Claude, Midjourney) to ship faster without cutting corners on quality.

I've conducted 30+ technical interviews, won a Gold Trophy at Ukrainian Design: The Very Best Of, and independently built a fully functional mobile app end-to-end using AI-assisted development tools.
